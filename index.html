ABOUT ME

I am Ying Zhang, an undergraduate student pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Statistics at the University of Nottingham, expected to graduate in May 2026. I maintain an overall GPA of 3.95, placing me in the top 5% of my class.

My research interests span simulation-based inference, neural posterior estimation, machine learning for credit risk analysis, and mathematical modeling. I have led multiple research projects funded by EPSRC and have co-authored papers under review at top-tier journals.

I have extensive experience in mathematical modeling competitions, having won multiple national prizes including 1st Prize in the Mathematical Contest in Modeling (2023) and Outstanding Prizes in 2024 and 2025. My work focuses on applying advanced machine learning techniques to real-world problems in finance, tourism optimization, and autonomous systems.
